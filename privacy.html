Zinrex takes user privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share personal information in accordance with KVKK (Turkey) and GDPR (EU) standards.

1. Collected Data

  • Identity: Full name, username, date of birth, gender, age verification
  • Contact: Phone number, email address, country
  • Profile Content: Photos, comments, likes, gifts, subscription info
  • Financial Data: ZingCoin transactions, in-app balance, payment info
  • Technical Data: Device ID, IP address, session logs, app usage stats
  • Advertising & Analytics: Ad interactions, rewarded video usage, analytics data

2. Purpose of Data Use

  • User account creation, management, and verification
  • Social interactions, messaging, likes, and gifting
  • Premium and subscription feature management
  • Fraud and abuse prevention
  • Service improvement and performance analysis
  • Displaying ads and distributing rewards
  • Legal compliance

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • All data is securely stored on Firebase Firestore and Firebase Storage.
  • Personal data is retained as required by law or as long as necessary for processing.
  • When a user deletes their account, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Data Sharing

  • Zinrex does not sell or share personal data without consent.
  • Data may only be shared with official authorities as required by law.
  • Service providers: payment processors (Papara), cloud storage (Firebase), ad networks, and analytics providers

5. User Rights

  • Request to know whether their personal data is processed
  • Access, correct, or update their data
  • Delete their account and all personal data
  • Manage subscription and ad preferences
  • Object to data processing

Requests can be made via zinrex.app@gmail.com or in-app options.

6. Users Under 18

  • Zinrex is not intended for individuals under 18.
  • Accounts identified as under 18 will be deleted immediately.

7. Security

  • All data transfers use SSL/HTTPS
  • Technical and administrative measures are in place to prevent unauthorized access

8. Contact

For privacy inquiries or requests: zinrex.app@gmail.com
