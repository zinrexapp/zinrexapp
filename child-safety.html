Child Safety Standards

Last Updated: January 2026

Zinrex is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We maintain strict safety standards to ensure that our platform remains a secure environment for all users.

Safety Standards URL

Our child safety standards are publicly available at: https://zinrex.com/child-safety.html .

1. Prohibited Content

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexual exploitation of minors
  • Grooming, predatory, or manipulative behavior
  • Harassment, abuse, or endangerment of children

2. Safety Measures

  • Users can report inappropriate content or users directly within the app
  • All reports are reviewed promptly by our moderation team
  • Accounts violating child safety rules are suspended or permanently banned
  • Repeat or severe violations result in immediate account termination

3. Reporting & Moderation

Zinrex provides in-app reporting tools that allow users to flag content or accounts related to child safety concerns. Reports are handled with priority and confidentiality.

4. Legal Compliance

Zinrex complies with all applicable child protection laws and regulations. Confirmed cases of CSAM are reported to relevant regional and national authorities, in accordance with legal obligations.

5. Cooperation with Authorities

We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies and child protection organizations when required by law to support investigations and ensure child safety.

6. Contact

For child safety concerns or questions regarding our CSAE prevention practices, please contact:
